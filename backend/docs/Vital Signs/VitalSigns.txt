Temperature:
A fever is considered when body temperature is >=38deg celcius.

It is also important to note that heart rate increases with fever in children. For every 1 deg celcius increase in body temperature, the heart rate increases by 10BPM


Pain Score:
Types of pain scores

Pain score >=4 to recheck the next hour.
Pain score < 4 to check per shift.
Offer analgesia where applicable.

Pain out of proportion is a red flag and to be reported!


Oxygen Saturation:
Oxygen saturation is measured via a finger probe on a pulse oximetry machine. 

Unless otherwise stated, oxygen saturation should be kept >=95% when patient is awake and >=93% when patient is asleep. 

Inform senior nurse / doctor is patient has desaturation.
